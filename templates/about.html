About Road Pothole Detection System

Revolutionary AI-powered solution for automatic pothole detection and analysis

Problem Statement

The Challenge

Potholes are a major problem in road infrastructure worldwide. Manual inspection is time-consuming, expensive, and prone to human error. Current methods require trained personnel to physically inspect roads, making it difficult to maintain comprehensive records and prioritize repairs efficiently.

Our Solution

Our AI-powered system automates pothole detection using advanced computer vision techniques. By processing images and videos, we can identify potholes with high accuracy, generate automatic reports, and help municipal authorities prioritize maintenance efforts more effectively.

Detection Algorithm Details

Multi-Stage Detection Pipeline

Step 1: Grayscale Conversion

Convert RGB image to grayscale for simplified processing

Step 2: Gaussian Blur

Apply blur to reduce noise and smooth the image

Step 3: Adaptive Thresholding

Convert image to binary format, adapting to local lighting

Step 4: Morphological Operations

Apply closing and opening operations to refine shapes

Step 5: Contour Detection

Find all contours in the processed image

Step 6: Filtering & Analysis

Filter by area, calculate circularity, and score confidence
